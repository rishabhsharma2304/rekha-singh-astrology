Services & Consultations

Each consultation is unhurried and personal. Rekha ji studies your chart before the conversation begins — there are no boilerplate readings here.

Kundali Reading & Birth Chart Analysis

A complete reading of your janma kundali — the placement of the navagraha across the twelve houses, the active mahadasha and antardasha, key yogas, and the practical implications for the period ahead.

Most seekers come for this when they want a foundational understanding of their chart, or when a specific concern — health, relationships, work, family — calls for careful study.

What's included

  • Detailed birth chart preparation (D-1, D-9 and other relevant divisional charts)
  • Analysis of current dasha and the next 2–3 years
  • Identification of supportive and challenging planetary periods
  • Practical guidance on areas of focus and timing
  • Personalised, dignified remedies — if any are needed

Typical duration: 60–90 minutes • In person or WhatsApp video

Kundali Matching (Guna Milan)

Classical 36-guna matching done carefully — not just the score, but the meaning behind each koota. Special attention to Mangal Dosha, Nadi Dosha and Bhakoot, with proper consideration of parihara where applicable.

Rekha ji also reads each chart independently to assess long-term compatibility beyond the numerical score — temperament, family environment, and the dasha periods both individuals will share.

What's included

  • Full Ashtakoota (36-point) analysis
  • Mangal & Nadi dosha review with parihara recommendations
  • Individual chart compatibility assessment
  • Honest discussion of strengths and areas of caution
  • Muhurat guidance for the wedding, on request

Typical duration: 60–90 minutes • Both charts required

Book this consultation

Vastu Shastra Consultation

Practical, grounded vastu — offered across three streams: Residence (from ₹200), Construction (from ₹500) and Education (from ₹500). Rekha ji's approach favours small, low-cost corrections wherever possible; she does not recommend breaking down walls unless absolutely necessary.

For Lucknow seekers, in-person visits are available. For others, a detailed floor plan over WhatsApp is enough to begin. Fengshui-style adjustments are layered in where the case calls for them.

What's included

  • Direction-wise assessment of layout, entrance, kitchen, bedrooms, pooja room
  • Identification of vastu dosha and energy imbalances
  • Practical, prioritised remedies — most are simple and affordable
  • Guidance for new construction, renovation, or classroom orientation, where relevant

Typical duration: 90 minutes (consultation) • Site visits available in Lucknow

Book this consultation

Gemstone Recommendation

The correct gemstone, the correct weight, the correct finger, the correct day — and the correct reason. Rekha ji recommends gemstones only when the chart genuinely calls for one. She is straightforward when a stone is not needed.

Where a gemstone is advised, she explains how to source a genuine stone (carat, clarity, metal, muhurat), and how to verify it before wearing.

What's included

  • Chart-based analysis of beneficial gemstones
  • Specific recommendation: stone, weight, metal, finger, day
  • Sourcing and authenticity guidance
  • Wearing muhurat and short purification puja instructions

Typical duration: 45–60 minutes

Book this consultation

Vedic Remedies & Puja Guidance

Remedies — mantras, daan, vrat, simple home pujas — drawn from classical Vedic tradition and matched specifically to your chart. Rekha ji believes a remedy should be sustainable: something you can actually do, week after week, with sincerity.

For larger pujas or yagya, she can guide you to trusted purohits in Lucknow and Varanasi.

What's included

  • Chart-specific mantra and stotra recommendations
  • Daan (charity) guidance — what, when, to whom
  • Simple home puja instructions, where appropriate
  • Referral to trusted purohits for larger ceremonies

Typical duration: 45–60 minutes

Book this consultation

Career & Business Astrology

For students choosing a direction, professionals weighing a move, or business owners considering a launch or partnership. Rekha ji analyses the 10th house, dasha periods, and key planetary transits to give you a clear sense of timing.

She does not promise outcomes — but she will tell you, honestly, what the chart suggests is favourable, what is not, and when to act.

What's included

  • 10th house and dashamsha (D-10) analysis
  • Dasha timing review for the next 2–5 years
  • Direction guidance for career choice, change, or business launch
  • Muhurat for important decisions, on request

Typical duration: 60 minutes
