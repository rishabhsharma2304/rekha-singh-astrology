About Rekha ji

A Quiet Practice, Carefully Done.

Rekha Sinha has been practising Vedic astrology from Arjunganj, Indira Nagar in Lucknow since 2022. Seekers find their way to her not for predictions, but for clarity — and many return.

Her grounding is classical Parashari, supplemented with Vastu, Fengshui and the remedial traditions of Lal Kitab where each genuinely helps. She is known for being patient with seekers, careful with remedies, and uncompromising about ethical practice — no fear-based selling, no expensive yantras pushed on the worried.

Consultations are available in Hindi and English, over phone, WhatsApp video, or in person at Indira Nagar.
